About us

Nestled in Varna, Bulgaria, a captivating aroma beckons passersby to a new donut shop, a haven curated by a team passionate about reinventing sweet indulgence. This dynamic team, led by pastry chef Dimitar and marketing maven Maria, fuses tradition with creativity. Their shop is a vibrant blend of modernity and local charm, from seaside-inspired decor to an open kitchen buzzing with inventive energy. What sets this place apart is their commitment to using local ingredients, supporting community businesses, and crafting donuts that are both familiar and delightfully unexpected. Classics like cinnamon sugar sit alongside bold creations like rosewater-pistachio and matcha green tea glazes. More than just a shop, it's a celebration of creativity and community. With each delectable bite, patrons experience the team's dedication to turning a simple pleasure into an extraordinary, flavorful journey.
